About InternHub

Empowering careers, one opportunity at a time

Our Story

InternHub was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between talented students and leading companies. We recognized that finding the right internship or job opportunity shouldn't be complicated, stressful, or time-consuming.

Today, we're proud to be India's leading platform for internships, jobs, and professional courses, connecting over 1 million students with 50,000+ companies across the country. Our platform has helped countless individuals launch successful careers and enabled companies to discover exceptional talent.

Our Mission

To make quality career opportunities accessible to every student and help companies build world-class teams by discovering the right talent.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted career development platform globally, fostering growth, learning, and success for millions of professionals.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, and inclusivity drive everything we do. We believe in creating equal opportunities for all and celebrating diversity.
